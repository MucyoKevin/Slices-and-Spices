Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Sunflower oil
  • 500 g Beef mince
  • 2 tbsp Fajita seasoning
  • 2 tsp Garlic granules
  • 2 tbsp Tomato puree
  • 400 g Kidney beans
  • 100 ml Water
  • Sea salt

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a saute pan over a medium heat. Add the beef and brown all over for about 5 minutes, breaking up with a spoon.
  2. Add the fajita seasoning, garlic granules, tomato puree, kidney beans, water and a sprinkle of sea salt. Cook for a further 5-10 minutes until the beans are hot and the meat is well cooked.
  3. Meanwhile, heat the tortillas according to the pack instructions (microwave, oven, gas flame or toaster all work!)
  4. Pile in the fillings (or let everyone help themselves) and serve!

Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • 300 g Cooked chicken, Approx 2-3 breasts.
  • 400 g Black beans, Drained and rinsed.
  • 340 g Sweetcorn, 1x tin. Drained and rinsed.
  • 1/2 Red onion, Peeled and finely chopped.
  • 2 tsp Fajita seasoning
  • 2 tsp Sunflower oil
  • 8 Large flour tortillas
  • 200 g Grated mozzarella

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the chicken, beans, sweetcorn, onion and fajita seasoning.
  2. Preheat 1/2 tsp of the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat.
  3. Sit one tortilla in the pan, sprinkle over a small amount of the cheese (about 25g â€“ no need to weigh but just as a guide!)
  4. Spoon on a quarter of the chicken and bean mixture.
  5. Sprinkle another 25g of cheese on top and sit another tortilla on top. Use the bottom of a clean saucepan to weigh down the quesadilla.
  6. Cook on that side for 3 minutes, then remove the saucepan and carefully flip over. Cook for another 3 minutes.
